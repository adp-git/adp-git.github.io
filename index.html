Tissue Scan

The process starts once our partners select and scan tissue slides using the scanners installed by Huron. After sensitive patient data is removed this data is then transfered to the Multimedia Lab at UofT.

Annotation

Pathologists can collaboratively annotate the slides on the moisaic level while being informed by the findings of our state of the art Artificial Intelligence model.

AI Model

Our computer vision model expedites the pathologists workflow by identifying the types of tissue that exists within the slides. This model since is then further improved by the annotations pathologists provide.
